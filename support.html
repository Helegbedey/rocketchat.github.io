Definitions

  • Pricing

    Prices shown are for annual contracts.

  • Point of Contact

    Support plans do not include end-users, once acquired a support plan the company must inform who will be the internal assigned account contacts for the support plan. Up to 3 contacts per company.

  • Self Help Resources

    Rocket.Chat provides user information for adminsitrators, end-users and developers on https://rocket.chat/docs

  • Access to product team

    For Premium Plan: One hour per month can be scheduled for consultation or problem solving with a core team member. Additional hours can be purchased for additional fees.

  • Critical Business Impact

    Critical issue occurring on Rocket.Chat preventing business operations to flow or a large number of users are prevented from working with no procedural workaround. In this case the first response time will be of 1 hour on Basic and 30 min on Premium.
    Example: data loss or corruption; system crashes; critical functionality not available; large number of users can't work.

  • Normal Business Impact

    Issue causing a partial or non-critical loss of functionality on production system. A small number of users are affected. In this case first response time will be of 12h on Basic and 8h on Premium.
    Example: some systems functions are not available; small number of users impacted; minor performance problems;

  • Minimal Business Impact

    Issue occurring on non-production system or question, comment, feature request, documentation issue or other non-impacting issue. In this case first response time will be of 24h on Basic and Premium.
    Example: product questions.
