Partner program

Become a Partner

Authorized partners can take advantage of a direct contact with the Rocket.Chat team in order to boost their sales.
From tech support to sales and marketing support, the Official Partner Program has three types of partnerships:

Technology Partner

Technology partners have advanced knowledge of how to customize Rocket.Chat’s code in order to be able to help Rocket.Chat clients to install and configure their servers to make Rocket.Chat scalable; perform brand theming of web, desktop and mobile apps;configure the admin panel; develop new features, integrations, bots as well as new applications for the marketplace and enterprise versions of Rocket.Chat’s code.

Value Added Partner

Value Added partners act as an intermediary between the client and Rocket.Chat by offering Rocket.Chat’s premium features (enterprise licenses or support plans). The partner will add consultation and training, as well as basic configurations and customizations, first and second level support.

Hosting Partner

Hosting partners offer their own infrastructure for Rocket.Chat deployment and must be able to offer basic support and handle first & second level support.
Rocket.Chat is responsible for the third level of support.
