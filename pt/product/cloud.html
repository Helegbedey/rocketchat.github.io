Rocket.Chat Cloud

The worry free way to affordably host, manage and scale Rocket.Chat

Rocket.Chat's robust and scalable Cloud, built on Docker and Kubernetes, gives you the benefits of an open source communication platform without having to manage and scale your own infrastructure and dedicated support team - saving you time and money so that you can keep bringing value to your customers.

Easy

Rocket.Chat Cloud is the easiest way to install and setup Rocket.Chat. It requires no technical knowledge about managing and securing servers, we take care of that got you, and can be completed in a couple of minutes.

Affordable

We believe in charging a fair rate for the resources your need rather than every user, giving you affordable and scalable team chat solution for tens of thousands of users.

Rocket.Chat Cloud requires no long term infrastructure or dedicated support team commitments giving you the most flexible affordable enterprise team chat solution.

Secure

Every cloud instance runs in it's own set of Docker containers with it's own database - isolated from other cloud instances. Under the hood day-to-day database operations are performed with restricted access to minimize risk.

We perform 24x7 monitoring on every instance and have configured hundreds of safety alerts to keep your team chat secure and fast. Your data up is backed-up up to every hour, to give you peace of mind in the case of an emergency.
