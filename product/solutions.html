FAQ

  1. What is Rocket.Chat Community?

    Rocket.Chat Community is a free and shared SaaS Rocket.Chat server for people all over the world to connect and communicate. With over 150,000 users, many who are technical, Community is the perfect place to seek help for your technical project.

  2. Can I use Rocket.Chat Community for my company?

    Yes you can but be aware that public channels are shared with everyone. Private channels are still private with whoever you share them with.

  3. What is the difference between Rocket.Chat On Premise and Cloud?

    Rocket.Chat On Premise is completely free to run on your own server. Rocket.Chat Cloud is our paid for hosted managed solution where we take care of hosting, security, upgrades, backups and more.

  4. Is Rocket.Chat On Premise completely free?

    Yes, you don't have to pay anything to run Rocket.Chat on your own server.
