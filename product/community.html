A free and open place for communities

Use Rocket.Chat Community to connect with your community and other communities.

Rocket.Chat Community is a completely free and open Rocket.Chat server hosted by Rocket.Chat.

We use it as our daily team chat server to discuss product development and #support users. If you have a question for us, post it in #general and we will get back to you soon.

Many people use Rocket.Chat Community for their daily team chat or just to try Rocket.Chat before installing it on their own server or starting a ] Rocket.Chat Cloud subscription.

Get involved

  • Star Rocket.Chat on GitHub

    Rocket.Chat is the leading open source team chat application. We have over 400 contributiors across our projects on GitHub. Star Rocket.Chat on GitHub and help others discover the platform.

  • Use and share Rocket.Chat

    We over 30 supported ways for you to download and install Rocket.Chat on PC, Mac or Linux. Setup your own server in minutes and share Rocket.Chat with your friends and colleagues who haven't made the switch to an open and customizable communication platform.

  • Fix a bug

    Rocket.Chat has a passionate and active community, get involved and help us squash bugs and manage issues for Rocket.Chat, Rocket.Chat.Electron, Rocket.Chat.iOS and Rocket.Chat.Android

  • Answer StackExchange questions

    Help us answer Rocket.Chat questions on StackExchange.

  • Write documentation

    You don't have to be a developer to contribute to Rocket.Chat. You can create new or update old Rocket.Chat.Docs articles or tutorials.

  • Write a blog post

    Write a blog post sharing your Rocket.Chat story and then tell us about it and we may repost it on our blog, include it in our newsletter or share it with the community on social media.

  • Give a talk

    Give a talk at a local meetup or conference sharing how you use Rocket.Chat or how you customized it to suit your needs.
