The worry free way to affordably host, manage and scale Rocket.Chat

Rocket.Chat's robust and scalable Cloud, built on Docker and Kubernetes, gives you the benefits of an open source communication platform without having to manage and scale your own infrastructure and dedicated support team - saving you time and money so that you can keep bringing value to your customers.

Disaster Recovery

One always has to be prepared for the worst. Rocket.Chat Cloud backs up your data up to every hour to give you peace of mind in the case of an emergency. Our infrastrcutre

Security

We implement state of the security on Rocket.Chat Cloud. Every cloud instance runs in it's own set of Docker containers with it's own database - isolated from other cloud instances. Under the hood day-today database operations are performed with restricted users to minimize risk. We perform 24x7 monitoring on every instance and have configured hundreds of safety alerts to keep your team chat secure and fast.
