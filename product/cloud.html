FAQ

  1. How long do the free trials run for?

    Every plan has a 30 day free trial. You can choose to cancel your plan anytime during the trial.

  2. When will I be billed after my trial finishes?

    You won't be billed automatically when your trial ends. Explicit permission to bill you and continue the plan will be requested.

  3. What is the billing period?

    All plans are billed monthly.

  4. When can I cancel a plan?

    You can cancel a plan at anytime and can continue to use your plan for the remaineder of your billing month.

  5. Can I upgrade from a Basic support plan to a Premium or Enterprise

    Yes you can upgrade to a Premium or Enterprise support plan, please contact sales with your enquiry.
