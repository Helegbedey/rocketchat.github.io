Get involved

  • Star Rocket.Chat on GitHub

    Rocket.Chat is the leading open source team chat application. We have over 600 contributiors across our projects on GitHub. Star Rocket.Chat on GitHub and help others discover the platform.

  • Use and share Rocket.Chat

    download and install your own server in minutes and share Rocket.Chat with your friends and colleagues who haven't made the switch to an open and customizable communication platform.

  • Fix a bug

    Rocket.Chat has a passionate and active community, get involved and help us squash bugs and manage issues for the Rocket.Chat server and Electron, iOS and Android clients.

  • Write documentation

    You don't have to be a developer to contribute to Rocket.Chat. You can create new or update old Rocket.Chat.Docs articles or tutorials.

  • Write a blog post

    Write a blog post sharing your Rocket.Chat story and then tell us about it and we share it on our blog, newsletter or social media.

  • Give a talk

    Give a talk at a local meetup or conference sharing how you use Rocket.Chat or how you customized it to suit your needs.
